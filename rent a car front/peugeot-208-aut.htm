Brief description

The new generation Peugeot 208 automatic perfectly complements the renewal of the brand and is establishes itself through its elegant and distinctive design. Compared to other models in its class, the appearance of the Peugeot 208 automatic 2021 brings a lot of youthful spirit and enthusiasm. Many extras will impress you in the interior, the biggest novelty is the latest version of the i-Cockpit 3D dashboard.

Expert opinion by Top Rent A Car

Entering the interior of the Peugeot 208 2021, the quality materials, the stylish layout and the ergonomics make an impression. The control of everything is intuitive and is done mainly through a 10" touch screen. The location of the rear seats is decent for such a small car. The driver's seat is comfortable and convenient. The model has navigation and DAB (Digital Audio Broadcasting) function, front and rear parking sensors, climate control , cruise control, LED headlights and a 180-degree reversing camera.The petrol engine and 8-speed automatic transmission ensure a pleasant and easy ride.
